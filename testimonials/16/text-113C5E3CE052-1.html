I kept pushing myself because l believed that if I worked harder it would get better and easier. After having a one on one call with Carey, she convinced me that "stronger not longer" was my better mantra. I slowly embraced the lower mileage and really ramped up the HITT training and glute workouts. I started watching what I eat, portion sizes and really trying to get more water. il suddenly saw a change in my body, my runs became easier and more fun, and I'm feeling better. My original goal when I started running over two years ago was to get in shape. Finally after two years I have the body I want and the mindset to go with it.
